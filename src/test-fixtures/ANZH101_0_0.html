ANZH101

Understand

  • Māori history is the foundational and continuous history of Aotearoa New Zealand.

Know

  • Māori are tangata whenua. They were the first people of this land and have stories about their origins and arrival.

Do

  • Generate questions that reflect my curiosity about people and communities.
  • Make observations about how people have acted in the past and how they act today.
  • Listen to other people's stories and points of view.

Learning intentions

Ākonga will:

  • understand Māori were the first people of this land
  • read and listen to these stories

How will I know if I've learned it?

Ākonga can:

  • share or retell a story of origin or arrival
  • create a pepeha to show connection with people and places of the past.

Supervisors

As you work through this module, keep in mind that there are different versions of stories depending on who tells them.

Origins and Arrivals

Ko ngā Tīmatanga me ngā Taenga Mai

Welcome to ANZH101

In this module you will explore the origins and arrivals of the first people of Aotearoa New Zealand. You will learn about the stories that tangata whenua have passed down through generations.
