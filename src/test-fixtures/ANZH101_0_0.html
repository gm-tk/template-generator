Module Overview

We are learning:
  • About the histories of Aotearoa New Zealand
  • Key events in our shared history
  • The significance of Te Tiriti o Waitangi
I can:
  • Identify important historical events
  • Explain cultural significance of landmarks
  • Analyse primary sources from New Zealand history

This module explores the rich history of Aotearoa New Zealand from early Polynesian settlement to modern times.

Course Information

ANZH101 is a Level 1 course covering New Zealand histories. Assessment is through workbook activities and a portfolio submission.

Welcome to Aotearoa New Zealand Histories

In this module you will explore the rich and diverse histories of Aotearoa New Zealand. You will learn about the arrival of Polynesian navigators, the signing of Te Tiriti o Waitangi, and the events that have shaped our nation.
