Land Wars and Conflicts

The New Zealand Wars

The New Zealand Wars were a series of armed conflicts fought between 1845 and 1872.

Causes of Conflict

Land sales and cultural misunderstandings led to growing tensions.

Key Battles

  • Flagstaff War (1845-1846)
  • Battle of Rangiriri (1863)
  • Gate Pa (1864)

These events are often referred to as the Land Wars or the New Zealand Wars.
